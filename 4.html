Views on AI by Jack Ma(Founder of Ali Baba)

while AI can augment and enhance human capabilities in various domains, there are inherent qualities and skills that are uniquely human and cannot be fully replicated by AI. The future is likely to involve collaboration and synergy between humans and AI, with each leveraging their respective strengths to achieve better outcomes.
